About Me

Melinda Christie is a voice actor in the San Francisco Bay Area, California. Her voice brings a blend of youthful maturity and relatable warmth to any project. Colleagues and mentors have compared her conversational tone to a dependable big sister, combining youthful vibrance with a relaxed confidence. Melinda began her voiceover journey while working as a technical writer, where she wrote and narrated video explainer scripts. She has also voiced video game characters, including 2 roles in the fan-made Final Fantasy IX Memoria Project. Whether it's casual or formal, Melinda's mellow yet consistent tone is suited for a wide range of voiceover needs, bringing a level of professionalism you can depend on.
