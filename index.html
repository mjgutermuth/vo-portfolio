About

Melinda Christie is a versatile voice actor and technical writer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. With a dynamic range and a knack for bringing characters to life, Melinda delivers engaging performances across multiple voiceover genres.

As a voice actor, Melinda is known for bringing a blend of youthful maturity and relatable warmth to any project. Colleagues and mentors have compared her conversational tone to a dependable big sister, combining infectious energy with a relaxed confidence.

Melinda's voiceover is further enhanced by her background in technical writing for leading software companies. She excels at making complex information accessible, both behind the mic and on the page. Melinda often combines her skills to offer end-to-end solutions, from script development to final VO delivery.

Whether she's voicing a tutorial, breathing life into a character, or crafting clear technical copy, Melinda's goal is always to engage, educate, and inspire. Her versatility, creativity, and commitment to quality make her a valuable asset to any project.
